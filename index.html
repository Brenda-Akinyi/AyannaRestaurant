Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about reservations, menus, delivery, and more.

Do I need to make a reservation?
Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and holidays.
Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?
Yes! Our menu includes a variety of plant-based and gluten-free dishes for all dietary needs.
Can I host a private event?
Absolutely. We offer tailored packages for weddings, business meetings, and private dining experiences.
Do you deliver or offer takeout?
Yes. You can order directly through our website or through major delivery apps.
What’s the dress code?
Smart casual is perfect. We want you to feel comfortable and elegant.
